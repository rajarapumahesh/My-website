”Low Cost Sanitary Pad Product Manufacturing Machine” in Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi (”UNNATI” MAHOTSAV) and this Project/Machine designed under the UNNAT BHARAT ABHIYAN

We had the honor of presenting our project, "Making of sanitary pads using low-cost and eco-friendly machinery," at the national-level event ‘MAHOTSAV & EXPO UNNATI-Unnat Bharat Abhiyan’ held at IIT Delhi. Representing Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology under the Participating Institute Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (PIUBA), our project was selected among 75 innovative projects in the Basic Amenities category. The event provided a platform to showcase our work, connect with experts, and share the potential impact of our solution on improving sanitary pad access. This experience inspired us, fostered collaboration, and strengthened our resolve to develop impactful, sustainable technologies.
